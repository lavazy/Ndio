Privacy Policy

Introduction

NDIO ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Definitions

All definitions used in this policy align with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), including terms like “Personal Information”, “Processing”, “Data Subject”, etc.

2. Purpose of this Policy

This policy explains how Wennze Cloud Services trading as NDIO ("NDIO", "we", "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares your personal information in compliance with POPIA.

3. Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal data directly from you or from authorised third parties (e.g., resellers, public records, or service platforms), in a lawful and transparent manner.

4. Lawful Processing

We only process your data when:

  • We have your consent
  • It's necessary for a contract
  • It's required by law
  • It protects your interests
  • Or it's within our or a third party's legitimate interests

5. Special Personal Information & Children

We process sensitive data or children's data only:

  • With explicit consent
  • When legally required
  • If it's been made public
  • Or if it's necessary for a legal claim

6. Purpose for Processing

We process data to:

  • Provide fiber, LTE, VoIP, and related services
  • Manage customer relationships and billing
  • Handle address checks and network availability
  • Monitor service usage
  • Support customer service and technical support
  • Prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Conduct direct marketing (with your consent)

7. Accuracy

We aim to keep your information accurate and current. Please notify us of any changes

8. Storage & Third-Party Processing

Data may be stored securely in our systems or by trusted cloud and IT providers. We ensure third-party processors are POPIA-compliant

9. Cookies

Our website uses cookies for functional, analytical, and marketing purposes. You may disable them in your browser settings

10. Marketing Use

You will only receive marketing communications if:

  • You're an existing customer;
  • You've given us permission
  • Or you've shown interest in our services

You can unsubscribe at any time.

11. Retention

Your information is only kept as long as necessary or required by law. Once no longer needed, it is safely destroyed or anonymised

12. Failure to Provide Information

If required information is not provided, we may be unable to offer you services or enter into agreements.

13. Data Security

We maintain strong physical, electronic, and administrative safeguards to protect your data from misuse or unauthorised access

14. Breach Notification

If a data breach occurs, we'll inform you and the Information Regulator as required under POPIA.

15. Disclosure to Third Parties

We may share information with:

  • Your chosen ISP
  • Hosting and IT service providers
  • Government or legal entities when obligated

All parties are bound by confidentiality and data protection clauses.

16. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections or deletion
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw your consent

Contact our Information Officer to exercise these rights.

17. Changes to This Policy

NDIO reserves the right to update this policy. Any changes will be published on our website with the effective date.
