About NDIO

Connecting Africa to the future of high-speed internet

Our Story

NDIO is a South African internet service provider dedicated to delivering lightning-fast, reliable connectivity to homes and businesses across the nation.

NDIO was founded with a bold and inspiring mission: to provide fast, reliable, and affordable internet access to every individual, everywhere — and to play a vital role in Africa's digital transformation.

As part of the forward-thinking Wennze Group, NDIO operates with agility, innovation, and a deep commitment to excellence. Our team is fully remote-enabled, using modern collaboration tools to work efficiently from anywhere in the world. We've created a digital work environment so seamless that it feels just like being in the office — promoting productivity, teamwork, and innovation across borders. We're not just building networks — we're building the foundation for Africa's connected future.
